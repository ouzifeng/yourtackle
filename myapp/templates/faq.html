The form requires a few fields to be filled in:

  • Brand: The brand of the product you are selling

  • More Info: Use these fields to add any additional information like rod length, bait size, bite alarm colour etc. The more descriptive you are the better your chances of getting a higher offer
  • Condition: Choose the relevant dropdown
  • Description: Any other information you think is necessary
  • Image: The more the better!
