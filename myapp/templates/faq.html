Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Selling

Yes you do. Creating an account takes less than a minute, without an account you will not be able to list any products.

To create an account visit HERE

Listing a product is super easy. Make sure you are logged in and visit HERE

The form requires a few fields to be filled in:

  • Brand: The brand of the product you are selling
  • Category: Find the most suitable category
  • Name: The tackle items Name
  • More Info: Use these fields to add any additional information like rod length, bait size, bite alarm colour etc. The more descriptive you are the better your chances of getting a higher offer
  • Condition: Choose the relevant dropdown
  • Description: Any other information you think is necessary
  • Image: The more the better!

Watch the video below where we upload a Fox Flatline Sleep System

After you have listed your piece of tackle in your account, an email goes out to all buyers inviting them to make an offer. When a buyer makes an offer you will get an email notification. Follow the instructions in the email to view the offer

Tackle shops will give you either a trade in price, cash price, or both. You can see offers my logging in and going to:
My Account -> Offers Made

Click on the 'view offer' button on the product listing. On the following page you can accept an offer by clicking on the Accept Offer button. If a buyer has offered 2 prices, you can let them know which one you want - i.e. either the cash price or trade in - when they contact you

The moment you accept an offer, the tackle shop is sent an email congratulating them. In that email are your contact details and they will reach out to you shortly either via phone or email to arrange pickup.

You will also receive an email with the buyers contact information. If yo uwould prefer, you can also reach out to them first to make arrangements

Buyers are not individuals but well regarded tackle shops. We only work with the top tackle shops who have been around a long time and have an excellent reputation. These tackle shops are extremely customer satisfaction driven and have no intention of ripping off anglers for a few pounds.

We suggest checking out the reviews for the shop who wins the offer
